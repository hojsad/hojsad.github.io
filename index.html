Biography

After receiving my PhD in Public Administration, teaching as Assistant Professor at Yazd University for 9 years, and working as a member of Yazd Research and Technology Fund Board of Directors, I am working on Risk Management and Machine Learning. I have also worked as a consultant for several theses and dissertations on Finance and Management at the university.
